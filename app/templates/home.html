Welcome to our Carbon App! Our app is designed to be a tool that can help individuals and organizations identify their main sources of carbon emissions and take action in order to effectively reduce their environmental footprint. The features of our application are currently limited to calculating how much a person's travel habits emits of carbon dioxide equivalents (\(CO_{2}e\)).

Our team

In the section below, you can get to know our talented team of developers! Learn about their unique backgrounds, explore their curricula, and check out their LinkedIn and Github profiles to get a deeper understanding of their skills and expertise.

Methodology

If you are interested in delving deeper into the technical aspects of our application, you can learn more about the methodology and data sources we have used on the 'Methodology' page.

Carbon App Calculator

If you want to learn more about the environmental impact of your travel habits, you can check out our Carbon App Calculator. Here you will find information about the carbon footprint of different modes of transportation, and you can calculate your own carbon footprint based on your travel habits.

We hope you enjoy our app and that it will inspire you to make more sustainable choices in your daily life!

Meet our exceptional team!

Our team consists of 5 developers, who are all students at NHH - Norwegian School of Economics. Our shared passion for economics and IT has fueled our excitement about combining these two fields to create this Carbon App that we believe can make a significant impact on the environment. We are driven by a strong desire to make a positive difference in the world, and we recognize that technology can be a powerful tool in achieving this goal.

Our team is deeply committed to developing a solution that will promote sustainable practices and encourage both individuals and businesses to make environmentally friendly choices in their daily lives. We recognize the pressing need for action to address the challenges facing our planet, and we believe that our application can be a catalyst for positive change.
